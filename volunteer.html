Volunteering at Sankalp Foundation

Thank you for your interest in becoming a volunteer!

Volunteering at Sankalp Foundation gives you the excellent opportunity to do meaningful and valuable work, empowering the underprivileged women and children. Anyone can volunteer and make a difference to our Trust, regardless of age, skill-set, or background. Without people like you, we wouldn't be able to improve the lives of the women and girls of Maharashtra.

Requirements for volunteering at Sankalp Foundation

  • We prefer volunteers who are able to stay for a minimum of two months (but it's not mandatory).
  • A satisfactory level of English.
  • It is important to have a willingness to work, and be able to understand what we want. You also need flexibility because the work in India can change.
  • If you have experience and/or skills in sewing, dress-making, or tailoring, this would be good.
  • A background in psychology or counseling for Aadarsh and Nirbhaya projects.

To apply as a volunteer send an email to sankalp@foundation.org with your CV and a motivational letter.
